# Site Staff Guide

For tradies, supervisors, and anyone on site logging incidents.

## Logging in

You'll get an invitation email with a link. Click it, set your name and password — that's your account. You can sign in at `${APP_URL}/login`.

If you have MFA enabled, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app after your password.

## Filing an incident

Three ways. Use whichever's fastest where you are.

### From the dashboard

1. Open the app on your phone.
2. Tap **Report incident** (or go to `/incidents/new`).
3. Pick the job (or "Not job-specific").
4. Add a short title.
5. Describe what happened. Use the **Dictate** mic if your hands are dirty.
6. Snap photos — the camera opens directly. Multiple photos are fine.
7. **Submit incident**.

If you're offline, the report queues on your device and uploads automatically when you're back online.

### From a job's QR poster

1. Scan the QR on the site board with your phone camera.
2. Tap the link. The check-in form opens.
3. After check-in, tap **Report an incident** at the top.
4. Same form as above.

### One-page printable

Print [How to file an incident on your phone](site-staff/incident-card.pdf) and fix to the site board.

## Viewing your incidents

`/incidents/mine` lists every incident you submitted, with status:

- **Pending review** — admin hasn't triaged yet.
- **Open** — admin has triaged; corrective action may be assigned to someone.
- **Closed** — resolved.

## Your actions

If an admin assigns you a corrective action, you'll get a notification. View at `/actions/mine`.

- Tap an action to see what's required.
- When done, tap **Mark resolved**. Add a comment if helpful.

## Meetings

If you're an attendee, you'll get an `.ics` calendar invite. Click the link in the email to add it to your calendar.

After the meeting, you'll get a sign-off email. Tap the link, sign on screen, submit. Once all attendees have signed and the director approves, you'll receive the minutes PDF.
